# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Plant maintenance engineers, project engineers, shop floor operators, maintenance managers, and factory supervisors tracking machinery, maintenance schedules, breakdowns, and engineering projects.

## Product Purpose
Comprehensive Maintenance and Project Engineering Management System (MACE) to track, schedule, and organize industrial plant maintenance, engineering projects, technical drawings, breakdown logs, purchasing, and safety audits.

## Brand Personality
Industrial, reliable, precise, efficient, high-utility, modern engineering control room.

## Anti-references
Generic pastel SaaS templates, unreadable low-contrast text, decorative glassmorphism clutter, hidden icon-only menus, buried primary actions.

## Design Principles
1. Clear Operational Status: High contrast, immediate visual status feedback (healthy, warning, breakdown).
2. Speed of Action: High density of actionable data without cognitive overload, fast filtering and direct workflow entries.
3. Industrial Precision: Clean grid layouts, structured tabular alignment, strong typographic contrast.
4. Fault Tolerant & Reassuring: Clear feedback on critical data entry, updates, imports, and state transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG 2.1 AA target, high contrast text ratios (≥4.5:1), complete keyboard accessibility for forms and tables, visible focus rings.
